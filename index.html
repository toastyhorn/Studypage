An Exploration of Overwatch: A Symphony of Sound and Image

For this project, I will explore the world of Overwatch by Blizzard Entertainment, focusing on the concept of Affect vs Emotion by Mazzui.

Audio Atmosphere

Let's take a listen at the music played during Overwatch gameplay during the final moments of the match. This music sets gives the player an anxious and exciting feeling as the round is coming to an end.

Visual Spectacle

This video of Overwatch is the first video that the world saw. It is the trailer video and also the video shown at the beginning when you open the game. It showcases some of the main characters and has lots of emotion built in with the music and visuals.

Character Portraits

Explore the diverse cast of heroes in Overwatch, each character has an interesting and unique visual design and adds to the depth and emotion of the game.
